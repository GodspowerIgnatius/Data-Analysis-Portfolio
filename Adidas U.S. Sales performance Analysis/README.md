# **Adidas U.S. Sales Performance Analysis**

## **Project Overview**

This project analyzes **U.S. retail sales data for Adidas**, covering two full years of transactions across multiple regions, retailers, and product categories. The goal of the analysis is to uncover revenue trends, retailer performance, product profitability, and year-on-year growth to support better strategic decision-making.
Using **Microsoft Excel**, the dataset was cleaned, standardized, and modeled to produce an interactive dashboard highlighting key insights on revenue drivers, operating performance, seasonality, and sales channels.

---

## **Tools Used**

* **Microsoft Excel**
* **Power Query** (data cleaning & transformation)

---

## **Data Cleaning & Preparation**

* Used **Power Query** to correct date formats, standardize retailer and product entries, and remove inconsistencies.
* Resolved mismatched Total Sales values by creating a **Scale Factor** column and calculating **True Total Sales** for accurate revenue reporting.
* Created derived fields including **Month**, **Year**, and **Sales Method** to support time-based and channel-level analysis.
* Ensured data completeness by checking for missing values in key fields (Retailer, Units Sold, Price per Unit, Operating Profit).

---

## **Steps**

1. Loaded and cleaned all transaction records using Power Query.
2. Built derived fields to support trend analysis and time-intelligence KPIs.
3. Developed PivotTables for retailer analysis, regional distribution insights, YoY performance, and product-level profitability.
4. Calculated KPIs such as revenue, total units sold, operating margin, and YoY growth.
5. Designed a clean, interactive Excel dashboard with slicers for Year, Region, Retailer, and Sales Method.
6. Integrated summary KPIs, trend charts, seasonal patterns, retailer rankings, and product category breakdowns.

## **Insights & Recommendations**

1. The **West and Northeast regions** lead in revenue, profit, and margins. These regions offer significant growth potential.
2. **West Gear, Foot Locker, and Sports Direct** are the strongest-performing retailers, while others underperform relative to their size.
3. **Men’s Street Footwear** is the highest driver of total revenue and profit, suggesting strong demand and expansion potential.
4. **Online sales** show rising contribution and healthy margins, indicating opportunities to strengthen Adidas’ digital strategy.
5. Seasonality impacts monthly performance; targeted product drops and promotions can smooth out low-volume months.
